
# Super Store Sales Analysis

## Overview

Developed a retail sales analytics dashboard using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, discounts, customer segments, and shipping trends across multiple product categories and regions.

The project focuses on transforming raw sales data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards, KPI tracking, and advanced data modeling techniques.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Modeling
* Business Intelligence

## Key Features

* Designed interactive dashboards for sales, profit, discount, and shipping analysis.
* Created KPI cards to monitor overall business performance.
* Built drill-down reports for category-wise, segment-wise, and region-wise analysis.
* Developed DAX measures for advanced calculations and business metrics.
* Implemented filters and slicers for dynamic user-driven exploration.
* Analyzed customer purchasing behavior and product performance trends.

## Dashboard Insights

* Identified top-performing and underperforming product categories.
* Analyzed regional sales and profitability trends.
* Evaluated the impact of discounts on revenue and profit margins.
* Compared customer segments based on sales contribution.
* Tracked shipping performance and order distribution patterns.

## Business Value

* Improved visibility into sales performance through centralized dashboards.
* Enabled faster decision-making using interactive visual analytics.
* Provided insights into profitability drivers and growth opportunities.
* Supported data-driven retail strategy and performance monitoring.
